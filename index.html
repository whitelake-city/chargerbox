Charger box

Tools

  • a small saw
  • a common saw
  • a drilling machine
  • a delta sander or sandpaper
  • a couple of spring clamps or sth similar
  • a pencil

Material

  • plywood 5mm strength
  • plywood 15mm strength
  • wood glue
  • rectangular block of wood 5mm
  • rectangular block of wood 10mm
  • log or dowels

Print the stencils.

You will need to print the stencils on DIN A3 (297mm Ã— 420mm). Visit a copy shop to do so.

Cut the shapes to create stentils

Follow the outer edge of the stencils when cutting. Don't forget to cut the gap for the cables of the lid.

Use the stencils to saw the plywood.

Use the 5mm plywood for the top, front and side panels. Lay the stencils down on the plywood. Use a pencil to draw the lines a long the outline of the stencils. When sawing, use a bigger saw for the straight lines and a smaller one for the round edges and details.
Make sure to leave a bit space between the stencils, as when you saw you are taking material away.
